Variety of flavours on our menu

Espresso

An intense and rich coffee drink extracted from freshly roasted coffee beans.

Espresso has a rich flavour and strong aroma, ideal for lovers of full-bodied coffee.

Latte

A delicate and smooth coffee drink consisting of espresso and hot milk.

Latte has a pleasant milky flavour and is decorated with latte art to visually decorate your cup.

Cappuccino

A combination of espresso, hot milk and lush milk foam.

It has a balanced flavour with notes of coffee and milk, and its silky texture is a pleasure to drink.

Flat White

A smooth and velvety coffee drink consisting of espresso and hot milk.

Flat White has a balanced flavour of coffee and milk, providing a pleasant coffee flavour.
